Agribusiness Management Development Program (ABMDP)* designed exclusively for prospective CEOs of Farmers Producer Organizations (FPOs)

Agribusiness Management Development Program (ABMDP), a unique course for Prospective CEOs of FPOs offered by a-IDEA, ICAR-NAARM with the support of NABARD TS RO for the duration of 60 days fully residential. The Program is focused on the development of managerial skills among the candidates and assist candidates to get a job interaction with FPOs in Telangana state.


Eligibility Criteria:
Minimum Qualification


  1. Candidate must hold a graduation in any discipline from recognized universities.
  2. 40 is the maximum age limit to be eligible for this program.
  3. Candidate should know Telugu and English language.

Desirable Qualification


  1. Graduates/post-graduation in Agriculture & Allied Sectors, Engineering, Economics, Management, Commerce, B.Tech (Agriculture) & Veterinary in any similar stream of study
  2. Work Experience in industry / FPOs

Ready to take your agribusiness career to the next level? Don't miss this incredible opportunity to grow, learn, and lead!
